About

Krunker is an online FPS within your browser. (Though a client can be downloaded from Steam and Krunker's own website.) The gameplay loop is quite simple, run around and shoot enemies (It's an FPS game, so of course), though there are other gamemodes that can alter the gameplay. The game comes with it's own mod loader so you can load huds, textures (Textures are client-side, thus your weapon will not look different to other players (unless there's already a vanilla skin on the weapon))
